What We Offer

SERVICES

Financial Services
Tailor-made financing with preferential rates for ultra-high-net-worth individuals. Approval within 48 hours. Operating and financial leasing in COP and USD. Partnerships with private banks: Bancolombia Private, BBVA, Santander. Zero down payment options available (subject to credit approval).
Extended Warranty
Extended warranty up to 5 years or 100,000 km. Full coverage: engine, transmission, suspension, electronics and exhaust system. 24/7 assistance nationwide. Genuine factory parts guaranteed. Free flatbed towing service. Annual diagnostic at no cost.
Driving Programs
Exclusive driving experiences at Autódromo de Tocancipá and private tracks. FIA certified instructors. Closed circuits, high-speed handling, emergency braking and controlled drifting. Personalized sessions for supercar owners and corporate groups.
Concierge Lounge
Private lounge with personalized attention, champagne, caviar and specialty coffee tasting. Vehicle customization consulting (Ad Personam). Document management, registration and insurance processing. Valet parking and key delivery in ceremony room.
Private Events
Invitation-only exclusive events: model launches, private exhibitions, gala dinners and VIP track days. Priority access to limited editions and pre-sales. Networking with collectors, celebrities and professional drivers.
🌸 Flower Decoration
Make your delivery an unforgettable moment with our luxury floral arrangements. From romantic bouquets with Ecuadorian roses to imperial arches with hundreds of natural flowers. Options available: bouquets, cabin decoration, and LED surprises.

ABOUT LM AUTOS

Motorsport
LM Autos is an official partner of motorsport events across Latin America: Formula 1 Mexico, 6 Hours of São Paulo, and TC2000 Argentina. We sponsor local and national talent in GT categories. We organize pace car experiences at international circuits.
Museum & Heritage
Our private showroom in Valledupar doubles as a museum of automotive excellence. We showcase icons from the past (Lamborghini Miura, Countach, Diablo) alongside contemporary hypercars. Guided tours available by appointment.
News & Events
Stay informed about global launches, limited editions and exclusive calls. Annual events calendar: exhibitions at luxury shopping malls, charity galas and elegance concours. Subscribe to our premium newsletter.
Sustainability
Committed to a sustainable future: we invest in hybrid and electric supercars (Revuelto, Temerario). Carbon footprint offset programs per kilometer driven. Solar-powered offices with LEED certification. Partners with Rewilding Colombia.
About LM Autos
Founded in 2026 in Valledupar, LM Autos was born to revolutionize luxury car sales in Colombia. Our team has over 30 years of combined experience with brands like Lamborghini, Ferrari, McLaren and Porsche. Over 50 satisfied customers in our first year.
Club Members
Access to the LM Autos owners club: private events, maintenance discounts, invitations to global launches and guided driving tours across Europe and the United States. An exclusive circle for the most passionate.
